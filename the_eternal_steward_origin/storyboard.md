# The Eternal Steward Origin Short

Working target: vertical 9:16 cinematic lead-in, about 64 seconds if later animated, designed to hand off into gameplay footage.

Visual continuity:
- The Steward is an ancient brass-and-cyan automaton with a weathered metallic face, glowing cyan eyes, circular halo-ring, ceremonial machine armor, and cosmic-cyan chest core.
- Mood: serene, pitiless, patient, almost priestly. The Steward should feel like a caretaker that has outlived every person it was built to serve.
- Palette: luminous cosmic cyan, polished or lightly oxidized brass, clean teal-blue shadows, warm gold rim light, and occasional broad magenta gradients only when useful.
- Active image pass direction: brighter premium sci-fi card art, smooth painted surfaces, crisp silhouettes, balanced exposure, no dirty grain, no dust, no muddy shadows, and no random star-speckle texture. Small cyan circuitry, route lights, and Knowledge-orb lights are acceptable only when they read as intentional scene elements.
- No readable in-image text, numbers, letters, glyphs, or UI.
- The original darker exploratory pass is archived in `dark_pass/`; the active images are in `images/`.

Lore anchors used:
- The Steward's Vision: `Zeroth Mandate`, rearranging all allies across Imperial, Frontier, and Ascendant Eras.
- Flavor: "But who will watch over the plan in 10,000 years?"
- User-provided voice: Cultists undermine the plan; Scholars accumulate Knowledge but cannot act; humans vanish long before the plan is complete; the Steward claims only it can lead humanity to Era Ascendant and a new galactic empire in 1000 years.
- Foundry mechanics: face-down Ascendant future, Knowledge, plans, era rearrangement, Cultist disruption, Scholar analysis.
