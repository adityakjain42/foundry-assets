# The Mayor Origin Short

Working target: vertical 9:16 cinematic lead-in, about 64 seconds, designed to hand off into gameplay footage.

Visual continuity:
- The Mayor is an older charismatic statesman with swept silver hair, a severe profile, navy formal coat, warm olive skin, and Byzantium purple/gold civic accents.
- Painterly Foundry style, dramatic oil-painted sci-fi portraiture, visible brush texture, amber civic light, purple shadow, no in-image text.
- The Mayor should feel charming, controlled, and faintly dangerous, but never cartoon villainous.

Lore anchors used:
- The Mayor's Vision: Edge of the Knife, granting a Hero increasing Influence.
- Flavor: "Violence is the last refuge of the incompetent."
- Isha's Hall of Signatures and "quiet arithmetic of power."
- Traders controlling logistics, Scholars pricing civic order, and Intuits/Navigators steering the fold and minds.
- Hyperjump danger reports and official decrees that keep lanes open despite panic.
